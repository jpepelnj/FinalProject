MJ's Final Shot

Jordan's final shot as a Chicago Bull could not have come at a better moment. MJ shakes off Byron Russell for his final, game-winning shot to win the NBA Finals over the Utah Jazz.

The Flu Game

Michael Jordan shocks the world by putting up 38 points in a crucial Game 5 victory over the Utah Jazz all while having the Flu.

The Shrug

After recording his 35th point in the first half, Jordan gives his famous shrug back at the Chicago Bulls bench. He was just unquestionably the best player on the court; sorry he's not sorry!

The Jumpman Dunk

One of the coolest dunks ever. Not much else needs to be said.

Ehlo Shot

The 1989 East Conference Finals winning shot over the Cavaliers. MJ fades left and shoots right over Craig Ehlo. As Coach Doug Collins famously said “That play was give the ball to Michael, and everyone else get the f*** out of the way”.

The Up and Under

One of the best readjustments at the hoop of all time.

The Blind Free Throw

A blind free throw may be a bit arrogant, but he's MJ so it's alright.
